Mohammed Al Ahdal

Senior Associate,
Dubai, UAE

E. mohammed.alahdal@ritcapitalpartnersltd.co.uk

Mohammed is a senior associate in our corporate and M&A team, based in our DIFC office.

He advises private clients, real estate and property developers and organisations from the retail, lifestyle, leisure and hospitality sectors. Predominately advising private clients on submitting new project details to the Dubai Land Department (RERA), he also advises on registration and licenses, provides real estate and tenancy dispute advice and advice on breach of contract and employee rights.
