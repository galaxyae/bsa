Tyne Hugo

Senior Associate,
Dubai, UAE

E. tyne.hugo@ritcapitalpartnersltd.co.uk

Tyne is a senior associate based in the DIFC office.

Practicing since 2012, Tyne specializes in corporate law and related DIFC litigation and arbitration. He has worked on all types of cases including freezing orders, construction disputes, and acting for numerous banking institutions for the recovery of funds in terms of facility agreements. He also works on corporate related work including due diligences, drafting and review of agreements and advising on corporate structuring.

Tyne regularly issues legal advice and opinions corporate tax, VAT, employment laws, insurance law and damages claims.
