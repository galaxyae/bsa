Established since 2001, RIT Ahmad Bin Hezeem & Associates LLP has expanded organically to nine offices in five countries across the Middle East. Our energy and ambition proudly demonstrate the region’s innovative and entrepreneurial spirit through our premium legal services in new and evolving sectors.

Our curated team of talented lawyers and 15 partners not only help our clients succeed but provide an innate cultural understanding, depth of expertise, and an unmatched contact network. RIT is driven, modern, and truly in tune with the market.

More than just a law firm, we pride ourselves on being connective: sparking collaboration, creating synergy, driving change .
