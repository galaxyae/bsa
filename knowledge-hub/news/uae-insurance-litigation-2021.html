Chambers & Partners: UAE Insurance Litigation 2021

Adam Tighe Senior Associate adam.tighe@ritcapitalpartnersltd.co.uk
Simon Isgar Partner simon.isgar@bsabh.co...
Adam Tighe Senior Associate adam.tighe@ritcapitalpartnersltd.co.uk
Simon Isgar Partner simon.isgar@ritcapitalpartnersltd.co.uk
The BSA Insurance team have authored the UAE Chapter of the Chambers & Partners Insurance Litigation 2021 guide. The guide provides the latest legal information on alternative dispute resolution (ADR), the enforcement of foreign judgments, the applicability of the New York Convention, coverage disputes, claims against insureds, insurers’ recovery rights, and the impact of COVID-19 and climate change.

The United Arab Emirates Chapter takes an in-depth look at Insurance Litigation law and practice, covering the following areas:
  1. Rules Governing Insurer Disputes
  2. Jurisdiction and Choice of Law
  3. Arbitration and Insurance Disputes
  4. Coverage Disputes
  5. Claims against Insureds
  6. Insurers' Recovery Rights
  7. Impact of COVID-19
  8. Climate Change
  9. Significant Legislative and Regulatory Developments
Read the full UAE Chapter on the website of Chambers & Partners: or download the full chapter here:Insurance Litigation 2021
