The Road to 2030 - An Overlook on Saudi Arabia and its Business, Investment and Legal Implications



Date: Tuesday, March 2, 2021

Time: 10:00 - 11:00 am U.S. Central Time

Speakers:Rima Mrad, Partner and Jean Abboud, Senior Associate

About: With the strategic framework of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 created around developing public service sectors, reducing the dependence on oil revenue, and diversifying the economy, what does this mean for private companies?

To answer this, we will look at the main themes of Vision 2030, primarily focusing on the theme of ‘A Thriving Economy’ and discussing the impact on international competitiveness, Public Investment Fund, Foreign Direct Investment (FDI), and oil and non-oil exports.

Join us as we discuss the Vision Realization Programs created by the Council of Economic and Development Affairs (CEDA) and the new investment opportunities created in various sectors in the kingdom, including tourism, entertainment and hospitality, consumer and industrial products, energy and resources, financial services, life sciences, and healthcare, public sector, and technology, media and telecommunication, and the legal implications and challenges for private companies based in the kingdom.
