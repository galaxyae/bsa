Speaking at Interpol regional conference

Nadim Bardawil Partner nadim.bardawil@bsabh...
Nadim Bardawil Partner nadim.bardawil@ritcapitalpartnersltd.com
Speakerat the 1st regional conference on illicit trade and intellectual property crime for the MENA region

Hosted by Interpol, at theJumeirah Messilah Beach Hotel & Spa, Kuwait on 1 -3June 2015

Topic: Best Practices Between Law Enforcement and Rights Holders

Presenter: Nadim Bardawil

He addressed the best practices between law enforcement and rights holders to increase the effectiveness of IP related criminal proceedings.

The event was chaired by the Kuwait Minister of Interior and the honorary guest of the event was Lieutenant General Dhahi Khalfan Tamim, the Head of the General Security for the Emirate of Dubai.
