Russia & CIS Desk

We are one of the few leading regional law firms with the capability to provide comprehensive legal services to Russian speaking investors and businesses in the Middle East. Owing to the rapid development of special economic zones, government incentives and relaxation of foreign investment regulations, the Middle East offers vast opportunities for multinationals seeking to expand into the region.

We can assist with: 
  • Supporting investment projects, including investment structuring
  • Advising on corporate law, including establishment of subsidiaries, branches, joint ventures as well as administration of companies
  • Cross-border restructuring of assets
  • Supporting M&A, venture capital and private equity transactions
  • Advising on foreign trade law issues, including drafting, negotiating, concluding and terminating of contracts
  • Structuring transactions complicated by sanctions
  • Litigation and International Arbitration, including debt recovery and enforcement procedures
  • Financial restructuring, bankruptcy & insolvency
  • Analysis of the effective sanctions and their effect on the operations of companies
  • Advising on embargoes and restrictions
  • Updating existing agreements in light of restrictive measures, obtaining licences
  • Advising on employment and immigration law issues, labor litigation
  • Advising on international tax issues and tax planning
  • Advising on real estate and construction law
  • Clearance availability searches for trademarks, designs and patents
  • IP due diligence, asset audit and management
  • Family and inheritance planning
